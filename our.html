Our projects

Cheese plants

MACU LLC was created with the goal of establishing a cheese-based economy in Mongolia and supporting local cheesemakers in producing high-quality cheese to be sold both domestically and internationally.

MACU has designed and built small, easy-to-use cheese plants across the country. The aim is to train interested individuals in European cheesemaking techniques, produce cheese using regional milk, and then purchase and sell that cheese through MACU’s shops and restaurants.

MACU is planning to build agrohubs in different locations in Mongolia. These agrohubs will spread on a few hectares and will have a cheeseplant, a greenhouse, a barn and even more.
