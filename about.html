Our story

MACU exists since 2015, and aims at developping, producing and selling fine cheeses made from Mongolia’s nomadic pastural milk (cows, yaks, goats, sheeps, etc.)

Today, MACU has its own restaurant, as well as a coffee shop and soon a shop in Ulaanbaatar. Cheeses made by a nearby cheesemaker are sold in these locations and used in the restaurants cuisine. A cheeseplant model has also been imagined and built in two locations : near Ulaanbaatar (Bio Kombinat), and in Tsagaanul (Khüvsghül). Cheese production is set to start there in Summer 2026.

Meanwhile, bigger projets are in the works such as more cheeseplants all over Mongolia, an affinage center and the development of “agrohubs”...
