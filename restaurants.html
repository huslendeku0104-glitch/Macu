Our Restaurant

Situated in central UlaanBaatar, our restaurants is offering western style cuisine : a vast array of pasta, soups as well as some of the best burgers in the city.

The Fromagerie MACU restaurant opened in 2015, and most of its cuisine is made with Mongolian Cheese produced and buy by MACU.

If you don’t have the time for a full meal, daily baked goods and cakes as well as MACU cheese are also available.
